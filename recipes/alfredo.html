Alfredo

Quick Info

This Alfredo sauce recipe always turns out rich and creamy with lots of garlic and Parmesan flavor!

A delicious way to transform plain fettuccine pasta, roast chicken, or steamed vegetables!

Ingredients

  • butter
  • heavy cream
  • Parmesan cheese
  • garlic
  • parsley

Steps

  1. Melt the butter, then stir in the cream and simmer.
  2. Whisk in the cheese and garlic until heated through and smooth.
  3. Stir in the parsley.
